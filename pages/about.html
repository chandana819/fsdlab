About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to our Resume Builder project. With a focus on simplicity and efficiency, we aim to help individuals craft resumes that highlight their strengths and accomplishments in the best possible way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use tool that empowers you to showcase your skills, education, projects, and achievements seamlessly. We believe a great resume is the first step to success.

Our Features

Multi-Step Form

Fill in your personal and professional details step by step for better organization.

Resume Templates

Choose from a variety of modern and professional resume templates tailored for every industry.

Live Preview

Instantly preview your resume as you build it to see how it will appear to recruiters.

PDF Export

Download your resume instantly in PDF format, ready to send or print.

Profile Image Upload

Optionally upload a profile picture to personalize your resume further.

Data Saved in Browser

Your data is stored securely in your browser using localStorage. No sign-up needed.
